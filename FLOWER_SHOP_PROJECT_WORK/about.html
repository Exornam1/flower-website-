Rubby's Flower Shop

Our misson

At The Rubby's flower shop, we believe in slow growth with soul, and support that stems from the earth. When you create spaces with integrity and surround yourself with beauty, you can be calm, grounded, and empowered within. Peace of mind is important to us. That’s why we grow high-quality plants and greenery with no cut corners, and no middle-man required.Finally, you can decorate your spaces like you live your life: in routine, with care. Let our fresh foliage fuel a balanced mindset, brighten up your rituals, and add life to the ordinary.

Our Aim

We’re honored to enliven your spaces, nurture your routines, and root you in radiance. With the largest assortment of floral, fruits, and foliage in the industry, we’re always adding variety.

Think of us as your personalized plant store meets best friend and biggest fan. We grow, test, and ensure that your plant is vivacious, well-raised, and lasting so it elevates your spaces: every season, every time. Feel in control, creative, and inspired – your new plants were grown just for you.
